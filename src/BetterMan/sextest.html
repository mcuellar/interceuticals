A Brief Male Sexual Function Inventory

This inventory, developed at Harvard Medical School, provides you with a Confidential tool to measure your sexual performance. It also allows you to measure whether your situation has been improved by taking BetterMAN. Simply answer each question before and after taking 3 bottles of BetterMAN and then compare the pre and post scores to see if there is any improvement. If you see some (even minor) positive change, continued daily use of BetterMAN will result in ongoing improvement.
