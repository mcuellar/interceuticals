Animal Testing
To conduct this study, Lue fed a control group of animals a normal diet and the treatment group a 1 percent high cholesterol diet. All animals with a high cholesterol diet became impotent. BetterMAN was added to the drinking water of half the treatment group of animals, 100 percent of which regained the same level of sexual potency as the animals fed a normal diet.
